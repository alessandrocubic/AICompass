Article 21 establishes the obligations of providers of high-risk AI systems regarding cooperation with competent authorities. Providers must furnish all necessary information and documentation to demonstrate conformity with regulatory requirements when requested by a competent authority, ensuring that this information is presented in a language easily understood by the authority. Additionally, providers must grant access to automatically generated logs of the AI system, as specified in Article 12(1), as long as these logs are under their control. Any information acquired by a competent authority under this article is subject to confidentiality obligations outlined in Article 78.
