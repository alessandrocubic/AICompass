Annex VIII outlines the information required for registering high-risk AI systems under Article 49:

1. Provider’s name, address, and contact details.
2. Details of any person submitting information on behalf of the provider.
3. Authorized representative’s information, if applicable.
4. AI system trade name and identification details.
5. Description of the system’s purpose, components, and supported functions.
6. Basic description of data used and the system's operating logic.
7. AI system status (market, service, recalled).
8. Certification details, including notified body info.
9. Scanned copy of the certificate, if applicable.
10. EU countries where the system is available.
11. Copy of the EU declaration of conformity.
12. Electronic instructions (except for certain areas like law enforcement).
13. Optional URL for additional information.
