Annex IX outlines key details required for registering real-world AI testing under Article 60:

1. Unique ID: A Union-wide identification number for the testing.
2. Provider/Deployer Info: Contact details of the provider and deployers.
3. AI System Overview: Brief description and intended purpose of the AI system.
4. Testing Plan Summary: Main points of the testing plan.
5. Testing Status: Information on any suspension or termination of the testing.

This ensures transparency and traceability during AI system testing.
