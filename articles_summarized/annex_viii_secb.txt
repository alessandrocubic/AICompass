Annex VIII Section B outlines the information required for registering AI systems deemed not-high-risk under Article 49(2):

1. Provider’s name, address, and contact details.
2. Details of any person submitting information on behalf of the provider.
3. Authorised representative’s information, if applicable.
4. AI system trade name and identification details.
5. Description of the system’s intended purpose.
6. Conditions under Article 6(3) that classify the system as not-high-risk.
7. Summary of grounds for the not-high-risk classification under Article 6(3).
8. AI system status (market, service, recalled).
9. EU countries where the system is available.
