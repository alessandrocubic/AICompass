Article 108 amends Regulation (EU) 2018/1139 by introducing several new paragraphs across different articles. Specifically, it adds paragraph 3 to Article 17, stating that when adopting implementing acts related to artificial intelligence systems classified as safety components under Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, the requirements in Chapter III, Section 2 of that Regulation must be considered. Similar provisions are added to Article 19 (paragraph 4), Article 43 (paragraph 4), Article 47 (paragraph 3), Article 57 (subparagraph), and Article 58 (paragraph 3), all emphasizing the necessity to take into account the same requirements when adopting delegated acts concerning these AI safety components.
