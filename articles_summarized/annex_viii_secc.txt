Annex VIII Section C specifies the information that deployers of high-risk AI systems must provide for registration under Article 49(3):

1. Name, address, and contact details of the deployer.
2. Contact information for the person submitting information on behalf of the deployer.
3. URL linking to the AI system entry in the EU database by its provider.
4. Summary of the fundamental rights impact assessment findings conducted per Article 27.
5. Summary of the data protection impact assessment results as per Article 35 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 or Article 27 of Directive (EU) 2016/680, where applicable.
