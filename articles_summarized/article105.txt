Article 105 amends Directive 2014/90/EU by adding a new paragraph to Article 8, which mandates that for artificial intelligence systems identified as safety components under Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, the Commission must consider the requirements outlined in Chapter III, Section 2 of that Regulation when performing its activities as specified in paragraph 1 and when adopting technical specifications and testing standards according to paragraphs 2 and 3.
