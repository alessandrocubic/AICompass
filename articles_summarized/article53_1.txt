Providers of general-purpose AI models are required to maintain and regularly update technical documentation that includes details about the model’s training, testing processes, and evaluation results, adhering to the minimum information outlined in Annex XI for submission to the AI Office and national authorities upon request. They must also prepare and share relevant information and documentation with AI system providers intending to integrate the model, ensuring compliance with intellectual property rights and confidentiality while allowing these providers to understand the model’s capabilities and limitations. Additionally, they must implement a policy to comply with EU copyright law, specifically identifying and adhering to any reservations of rights under Directive (EU) 2019/790, and publish a detailed summary of the training content used for the model, following a template from the AI Office.
