sArticle 12: Record-Keeping Summary

High-risk AI systems must have the capability to automatically log events throughout their lifecycle for traceability. Logging should include:

1. Event Identification: Capture situations that may present risks or substantial modifications.
2. Post-Market Monitoring: Support post-market monitoring processes.
3. Operational Monitoring: Track system operations.

For specific high-risk AI systems, logs must include usage periods, reference databases, matched input data, and identification of individuals verifying results.
